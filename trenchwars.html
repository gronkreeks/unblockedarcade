TRENCH WARS

Two armies face off across no man's land. Advance your soldiers, deploy sappers to fortify the front, and storm the enemy fortress.

Controls

Click unit → select  |  Click blue → move  |  Click red → attack
Soldier: fights — buy with B (20g)
Sapper: builds — buy with S (30g)
  D = trench (free)  |  M = gold mine (50g, +15g/turn)  |  N = bunker (80g, ×2.5 defense)
Flamethrower: forces trench retreat — buy with F (60g)
  Defenders within 2 tiles counter-fire: need 3 FTs per 1 defending FT
Enter = end turn  |  Win by storming the enemy fortress.

What you'll learn

Builds tactical thinking, resource management & spatial reasoning — weigh when to advance, when to fortify, and how to control the gold economy.
