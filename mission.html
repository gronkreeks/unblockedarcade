Mission Statement

Education should be
accessible to everyone.

EduArcade was created to make learning a genuine and fun experience. My younger sister told me she was tired of the books, the study techniques and boring lessons. I took that to heart. I made EduArcade, a cost-free way to teach your child motor skills, typing, and strategic reasoning. This is a passion project, created to learn how to use HTML5 and learn to construct websites. I believe in high quality, fun games that all kids can play while still teaching them something so it's not just a waste of time.

Our three pillars

Accessible to All

EduArcade runs on all devices, with available games for mobile users. No installation, no login needed and no cost. If you have an internet connection, you can learn.

Play & Learn

Research shows that children absorb and retain skills more effectively when they are engaged and having fun. Every game here is designed so that the skill is the game and keeps them coming back and learning.

Skills

Every game is programmed from the beginning with education in mind, every little detail made to teach children time management, literacy, motor skills, design and most importantly, technology skills.

What our games teach

Motor Skills & Coordination
2-player games teach motor skills in a fun, competitive manner where children can make connections with others, while teaching them valuable motor skills, important for improving typing strength and and physical literacy.
Tag Boxing Sniper Star Clicker
Strategy & Critical Thinking
My games teach strategy and critical thinking, forcing players to plan ahead and create strategies that can be refined over time through critical thinking. For kids to grow into functional adults, they must be able to organize and plan their routines. These games teach this before that responsibility comes alive, providing a significant advantage as an adolescent.
Battle Chess Survive
Literacy & Technology
These games teach core digital literacy skills, that are extremely important in the modern world. Skills such as typing are essential in this world, being taught digital literacy and how to navigate the internet is an extremely important strength to have as the world is transformed by computers and AI.
Type Quest
