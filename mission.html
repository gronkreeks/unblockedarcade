Mission Statement

Education should be
accessible to everyone.

EduArcade was created to make learning a genuine and fun experience. My younger sister told me she was tired of the books, the study techniques and boring lessons. I took that to heart. I made EduArcade, a cost-free way to teach your child motor skills, typing, and strategic reasoning. This is a passion project, created to learn how to use HTML5 and learn to construct websites. I believe in high quality, fun games that all kids can play while still teaching them something so it's not just a waste of time.

Our three pillars

Accessible to All

Every game runs instantly in any browser on any device — phone, tablet, or desktop. No installation, no account, no cost. If you have an internet connection, you can learn.

Learning Through Play

Research consistently shows that children absorb and retain skills more effectively when they are engaged and having fun. Every game here is designed so that the skill is the game — not a worksheet bolted on top of it.

Real, Measurable Skills

We are deliberate about what each game teaches. Motor skills, critical thinking, literacy, strategic reasoning — these are transferable skills that matter inside and outside the classroom.

What our games teach

Motor Skills & Coordination
Rapid physical response, hand-eye coordination, spatial awareness and agility — the foundations of physical literacy that support both sport and everyday tasks.
Tag Boxing Sniper Star Clicker
Strategy & Critical Thinking
Forward planning, resource management, logical reasoning and adaptive decision-making under pressure — skills that translate directly to mathematics, science and everyday problem-solving.
Battle Chess Survive
Literacy & Technology
Keyboard fluency, typing speed and accuracy — core digital literacy skills for every student in a world where written communication happens on a screen.
